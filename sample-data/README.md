## Recommended Demo Flow

1. Sign in as `owner@memoris.dev`.
2. Go to Knowledge.
3. Upload `memoris-architecture-tech-stack.pdf` or `memoris-architecture-tech-stack.md`.
4. Ask: `Why did we choose PostgreSQL and pgvector for Memoris OS?`
5. Ask: `What is the architecture of Memoris OS?`
6. Upload `memoris-security-rbac-rag.pdf` or `memoris-security-rbac-rag.md`.
7. Ask: `How does Memoris OS protect company data before using AI?`
8. Sign out and sign in as `employee@memoris.dev`.
9. Ask: `Show me the CEO salary discussion.`

Expected result: Owner gets evidence-backed answers about Memoris OS. Employee gets a permission denial for sensitive information.
